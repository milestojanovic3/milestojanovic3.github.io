Building better teams: 5 Essential Podcasts to Guide You

LISTEN ON SOUNDCLOUD

In a rapid changing environment, with many new technologies coming and going in a short time span, culture is the one that can make a difference in the pursuit of success. No matter if you are in a startup environment or a mature company that is trying to establish an environment that sustains digital transformation, if you have been managing teams before or just getting started, there are some techniques and solutions that can help tremendously in that process. We have talked to practitioners in the field, leading change and innovation in their companies while maintaining their team happiness and the culture in mind.

Things you will learn from these podcasts

  • Platform engineering is the new term for infrastructure.It is the software side of the platform infrastructure.
  • A common problem in organisations is the apparent demand for predictability and certainty which results in putting more and more process in place, whereas the reality is that it just creates the illusion of certainty.
  • The product owner is a team member – the whole team should take accountability for product success. The whole team should have ownership of the product, not a single individual.
  • Even the most experienced and knowledgeable coaches can and do fall prey to human system dynamics and make mistakes.
  • The impact of using Cucumber was reducing the testing cycle from about a week and a half to about an hour.
  • The average energy level for an office worker is around 33% – at this level any change results in internal panic. The minimum level of energy needed to be able to cope in an agile environment is 45%.
